This is my current policy of writing recommendation letters.

*This version: v.1.1. Date: 2026-12-17*

# Recommendation Letters

## Phd reference letter policy

I'm writing a PhD support letter if

* you took at least of one of my advanced classes (e.g. DA3, DA4, Applied Econ)
* you got A at least at one of them, and A or A- for the rest
* OR you worked for me as an RA

## Job reference letter policy

I'm writing a job application support letter if

* you took at least of one of my advanced classes (e.g. DA3, DA4, Applied Econ)
* you got A or A- at least at one of them
* OR you worked for me as an RA 

## To submit

Please submit 

* CV
* Grades for all my classes, and may resend the best project/ assignment you did and/or link to repo
* your motivation letter 
* GPA / expected GPA
* Deadline(s)

For Phd

* Please give a list of programs that I'll need to send, and the deadline for the first.

For jobs

* Please provide a note on how your performance at my classes or as my RA/TA is relevant for the job.


Godspeed.
