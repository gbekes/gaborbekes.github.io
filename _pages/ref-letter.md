This is my current policy of writing recommendation letters.

*This version: v.1.2. Date: 2026-04-19*

# Recommendation Letters

## PhD reference letter policy

I'm writing a PhD support letter if

* you took at least one of my advanced classes (e.g. DA3, DA4, Applied Econ)
* you got A at least in one of them, and A or A- for the rest
* OR you worked for me as an RA

## Job / internship reference letter policy

I'm writing a job or internship support letter if

* you took at least one of my advanced classes (e.g. DA3, DA4, Applied Econ)
* you got A or A- at least in one of them
* OR you worked for me as an RA

## How to submit

Please send me **one email** with everything attached, or a link to **one folder** (Dropbox / Drive / OneDrive) containing:

* **CV**
* **Motivation letter** / statement of purpose
* **Application info**: Simple text (txt, .md) document with core info for each program/position:
  * **GPA** (or expected GPA)
  * **Grades** for all my classes
  * **GitHub repo** -- link to your repo of your best project/assignment, or the file itself (*optional*)
  * **Target / addressee** — who the letter should be addressed to (e.g. "Admissions Committee", "Hiring Committee, XYZ Team")
  * **Link** to the posting or program page
  * **Deadline** (absolute date)
  * **How the letter is delivered** — I send it directly to you, you upload it to a portal, or a confidential form link you paste here
  * Whether a **signed and scanned** version is required (default: I send a signed PDF)

Please name files clearly, e.g. `CV_LastName.pdf`, `Motivation_LastName_Program.pdf`, `application-info_LastName.md`, 

**For PhD applications**

Add this to the application info text:

* Your interests, fields, plans. 
* Why you think you are ready for a phd program, to carry out research
* If there are several programs, list each one (addressee, link, deadline, delivery) in a short table or bullet list. Mark the earliest deadline clearly.

**For jobs and internships**

Add this to the application info text:

* A short note (3–5 sentences) on **how your performance in my classes or as my RA/TA is relevant for the role**. This helps me tailor the letter rather than write a generic one.

Godspeed.
